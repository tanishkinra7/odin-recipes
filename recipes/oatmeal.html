Oatmeal with Strawberries

strawberry oatmeal is definitely healthy. It's also gluten-free (make sure you're using gluten-free oats) and if you use a milk alternative, it can easily be made dairy free as well.

Ingredients used

  • 50g Quaker Oats
  • 250ml milk
  • 1 cup Strawberries

Steps

  1. Pour 250ml of skim milk into a bowl
  2. Add 50gms of oats
  3. microwave it for 2 minutes
  4. Serve it with a cup of strawberries
